A Premium Boutique Hotel in the heart of Trivandrum.

Serene offers you a blend of luxury and affordability while choosing rooms. We have well maintained, spacious rooms, with options for single and double occupancy. All the rooms have free wifi internet connectivity, running hot & cold water facility, direct dialing telephones and satellite TV channels. The rooms at The Grand Hotel have been designed keeping you and your comfort in mind. Discover an atmosphere that combines the finesse of a luxury hotel in the tastefully done rooms, with wooden flooring, and subtle lightings all adding to the ambiance of calm & style. Our rooms are designed in art deco with modern amenities like mini bar, safe etc.. ideal for leisure and business traveler alike. A thoughtful combination of elegance and functional excellence, each of the rooms satisfy your needs. Your pleasant memories will linger through out your life.

Spices
